Eye Controlled Mouse

About the Project

This project demonstrates how to use eye-tracking technology to control a computer mouse. By leveraging computer vision and machine learning tools like Mediapipe and OpenCV, this innovative system allows users to interact with their computer using just their eyes.

How It Works

  • The camera captures a live video feed of your face and eyes.
  • Using Mediapipe's FaceMesh, the system tracks your eye movements in real-time.
  • The cursor moves based on your iris position, and blinking triggers mouse clicks.
